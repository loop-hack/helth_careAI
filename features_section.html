Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SentiCare

Is SentiCare a real human therapist?

No, it's an AI trained with therapy techniques like CBT. It can escalate to real therapists if needed.

Is my data private and safe?

Yes. We use end-to-end encryption and follow HIPAA & GDPR regulations.

Can I talk in Hindi or other languages?

Yes! SentiCare supports multilingual conversations.

What if I feel worse after using it?

If signs of serious mental distress are detected, SentiCare suggests urgent human help.
