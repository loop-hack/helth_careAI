Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2023 | Last Updated: June 15, 2023

Important: This Privacy Policy describes how MindEase AI Therapy ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your personal information when you use our AI-powered mental health services. This policy complies with GDPR, CCPA, HIPAA (for US users), and other applicable privacy laws.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email, phone number, date of birth, gender (optional)
  • Health Information: Mental health history, current symptoms, medications, treatment goals
  • Therapy Data: Session transcripts, mood logs, journal entries, progress assessments
  • Payment Information: Billing address, payment card details (processed securely by our payment processor)

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Session duration, features used, interaction patterns with our AI
  • Cookies and Tracking: Session cookies, analytics cookies (see our Cookie Policy)

1.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information from:

  • Healthcare providers (with your explicit consent)
  • Payment processors regarding subscription status
  • Integrated wellness apps (if you choose to connect them)

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Primary Uses

  • Deliver and personalize AI therapy sessions
  • Develop treatment plans and track progress
  • Improve our AI models through machine learning
  • Process payments and maintain your account
  • Provide customer support

2.2 Secondary Uses

  • Conduct anonymized research to improve mental health services
  • Prevent fraud and ensure service security
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Send service-related communications (not marketing)

2.3 Legal Bases for Processing (GDPR)

  • Performance of Contract: For providing our services
  • Consent: For sensitive health data and optional features
  • Legitimate Interests: For service improvement and security
  • Legal Obligation: When required by law

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 Service Providers

We share data with:

  • Cloud hosting providers (data encrypted at rest and in transit)
  • AI model training platforms (with strict data protection agreements)
  • Payment processors (never storing full payment details)
  • Customer support platforms

3.2 Legal Disclosures

We may disclose information when:

  • Required by subpoena, court order, or other legal process
  • Necessary to prevent imminent harm to yourself or others
  • Required by healthcare regulations in your jurisdiction

3.3 Business Transfers

In case of merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your data may be transferred with appropriate protections.

4. Data Security

4.1 Protection Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for all therapy sessions
  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • TLS 1.2+ for all data transmissions
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Access controls and audit logging

4.2 Data Breach Protocol

We will notify affected users and relevant authorities within 72 hours of becoming aware of any breach involving personal data, as required by law.

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Control

You may:

  • Access your personal data through your account dashboard
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of non-essential data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Export your therapy data in standard formats
  • Withdraw consent for optional processing

5.2 Communication Preferences

Manage email notifications in your account settings. We do not send marketing communications without explicit opt-in.

5.3 Exercising Your Rights

Submit requests to our Data Protection Officer at the contact information below. We will respond within 30 days.

6. International Data Transfers

For users outside the United States:

  • EU-US Data Privacy Framework certification
  • Standard Contractual Clauses for other international transfers
  • Data processed only in jurisdictions with adequate protections

7. Data Retention

  • Active therapy records: 7 years from last use (clinical retention requirement)
  • Account information: Until deletion request (with 30-day backup retention)
  • Anonymized research data: Indefinitely

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not designed for users under 13. For users 13-17, parental consent is required for processing health data.

9. Changes to This Policy

We will notify users of material changes via email and in-app notice 30 days before changes take effect.
